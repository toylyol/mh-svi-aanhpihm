"MinorityHealthSVI_DataDictionary_2018.csv" is the MH SVI data dictionary. It is available [online](https://www.minorityhealth.hhs.gov/minority-health-svi/).

"mh_svi_county_2018.csv" is the MH SVI data, which is only available at the county level.


"CDC_SVI_2018_DD.xlsx" is the 2018 SVI data dictionary, and it was retrieved from [GitHub](https://github.com/cymonegates/SVI_R_Script). 

"SVI2018_US.csv" is the US-wide 2018 SVI Census tract dataset.

"SVI2018_US_COUNTY.csv" is US-wide 2018 SVI county dataset.


"covid_confirmed_usafacts.csv" is the confirmed COVID cases data on [USAFacts.org](https://USAFacts.org/issues/cornavirus/) as of April 17, 2023.

"covid_deaths_usafacts" is the COVID deaths data from USAFacts.org. See [Cases and Deaths section](https://usafacts.org/visualizations/coronavirus-covid-19-spread-map/).

"covid_deaths_usafacts" is the county population data form USAFacts.org. See [Cases and Deaths section](https://usafacts.org/visualizations/coronavirus-covid-19-spread-map/).

USAFacts.org has a detailed [COVID data methodology page](https://usafacts.org/articles/detailed-methodology-covid-19-data/). It states that "cases, deaths, and per capita adjustments reflect cumulative totals since January 22, 2020."


"Post-COVID_Conditions" is the CDC's Long COVID Household Pulse Survey data as of April 19, 2023. See [online](https://data.cdc.gov/NCHS/Post-COVID-Conditions/gsea-w83j).
